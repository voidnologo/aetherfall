## Key Design Decisions

- **Story 03 = magic story, next story = combat story.** Each story showcases a different game facet. Invisible to reader, vivid to anyone who reads the rulebook.
- **Record of Lodoss War model.** Great story first. Makes you want to play the game second.
- **Sera's river vs. Aldric's lattice.** Two metaphors for the same force — wild casting as swimming in a current, scholarly casting as building a vessel for the current to fill. Neither is better.
- **Sera's Amplify is almost involuntary.** The magic in her responding to the artifact. She tears herself free. This is the turning point — the wild caster developing caution. Not fear, but awareness of the riptide.
- **Exhaustion accumulates across chapters, not scenes.** Sera's depletion is a thread from Ch 3 through Ch 7 (17 hours of sleep). The reader feels finite resources.
- **Kael's revolver as mirror to Sera's casting.** Both are environment-dependent. The non-caster's experience of the Aether/Galvanic balance.
- **The trust crack is stated, not resolved.** Kael calls Mira's filtering. Neither is wrong. The argument continues in future stories.

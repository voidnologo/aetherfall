## Key Design Decisions

- **Neutral territory as default:** Most of the world is mundane. Zones are the exception. This makes entering a zone feel significant rather than routine.
- **Veil produces ecosystems, Engine produces objects:** Even wild Engine manifestations are things (devices, structures) not transformed landscapes. This asymmetry is core to the world's identity.
- **Old-world tech is neutral:** Only post-Tear innovations that harness the Engine create Galvanic zones. A coal foundry is just a building.
- **Artifact is inaccessible:** The container can be seen but not reached — spatial loop defense. This creates a deadlock (all factions monitoring, none able to claim it) and sets up future story hooks around finding the key.
- **Ironwork escalates to lethal force:** Out in the Wilds, beyond authority's reach, the Collective authorized killing a chartered Society. This shatters the pretense of professional competition and establishes real stakes.
- **Sequential writing, not parallel:** Wrote all 10 chapters in order to maintain continuity. Needs editing pass but shouldn't have seam issues.

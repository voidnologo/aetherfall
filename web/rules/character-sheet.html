Character Sheet

Build your character online or grab a blank sheet to fill by hand

The character builder does all the math for you — click attributes, pick skills from dropdowns, select spells, and it calculates targets, exhaustion costs, and health tracks automatically. When you’re done, hit Print to get a clean two-page character sheet.

If you prefer pen and paper, grab the blank printable sheet and fill it in at the table.

Character Builder

An interactive form that walks you through character creation. Click attributes to set them, pick skills from dropdowns with auto-calculated targets, select your casting path and spells, and fill in weapons, armor, society details, and contacts. Everything prints as a formatted two-page character sheet.

FeatureWhat It Does
AttributesClick −3 to +3 for each stat; derived values update live
SkillsPick from all 27 skills; stat, target, and pool budget calculated automatically
MagicChoose Scholarly (pick individual spells) or Wild (pick 2 schools, get all spells)
EquipmentType in weapons, armor, and inventory; soak and hinder auto-totaled
PrintGenerates a clean two-page sheet matching the blank template

Chapter 18

GM Tools

Interactive tools for running the game at the table

These tools run entirely in your browser — no server, no login, no internet required after the page loads. Open them on a laptop or tablet at the table and they’re ready to go.

Zone Tracker

Track Aetheric and Galvanic accumulation in real time. Set your area’s baseline, click buttons as spells are cast and exotic weapons fire, and the tracker automatically calculates the current Reliability and Casting Target modifiers — no mental math required.

FeatureWhat It Does
Baseline controlSet the area’s permanent zone level (−30 to +30)
Spell buttonsOne click per spell tier (Weak +1 through Spectacular +4)
Weapon buttonsOne click per Galvanic weapon shot (Rating 1 through 3)
Live effectsShows Reliability and Casting modifiers, plus example weapon and caster
Decay controlsSimulate 1, 5, or 10 minutes of decay (1 point per minute)
Activity logColor-coded history of every shift and decay

Open Zone Tracker →

Combat Tracker

A visual timing track for combat. Add players and NPCs to the roster, drag their tokens to the count where their action resolves, and advance the timeline as the fight unfolds. The track extends infinitely — it always shows 30 counts ahead of the current position.

FeatureWhat It Does
RosterAdd PCs and NPCs with names; auto-assigned color tokens
Drag & dropDrag tokens to any count on the timeline, or between counts
Advance 1 CountStep forward one count at a time
Next ActionSkip ahead to the next count that has a token
Infinite timelineTrack auto-extends as combat progresses; 30-count visible window
Unplaced stagingTokens waiting for placement sit in a staging area below the track
